Where to sell my products

Raising pigs is a wonderful rewarding work,but it won't lead to a viable farm enterprise unless we develop an effective marketing strategy. In this page we'll provide you with the best farm markets for your small,middle or large scaled farms.

Before you start marketing your pork to potential buyers,it is worth it to ensure that you have with you all relevant licenses from relevant authorities,this is especially so if your target markert is restaurants or other businesses. Your products should be certifed to be fit for human consumption so as to sell well. Below are some methods of selling your pigs to the market.

When it comes to selling meat(pork in this case) there are several ways to make your product reach the buyer. A frequently used method is to sell a whole animal through a slaughter house/abbatoir which inspects the meat and some process the meat then sells to butcheries who sell to customers. For small and medium scale farmers there are slaughter slabs in towns all over the country for one to slaughter their pigs.

Another sale method is to sell individual products to the buyers. You can have your meat processed then sell individual cuts wholesale or retail. You can sell this individual cuts to customers who want special pork products or even restaurants which order specific pork products for their recipes. If you wish to sell by this method it is best to have a good coomercial freezer. You can make more money by selling through this method. However this method is most suitable for large scale farmers.

You can also sell a live animal directly to a customer which is a common occurence during festivities. You can either deliver to the locker for processing for the customer or let the customer butcher for themselves.

If you are wondering where to sell your matured ready to slaughter pigs this website will help connect you with your product's potential buyers.

Near the major cities in Kenya i.e Nakuru,Nairobi there is a large market especially for large and some medium scale farmers.Below are a number slaughter houses to sell your pigs.

Farmer's Choice Limited

Farmer's Choice is the largest abattoir in Kenya located in the outskirts of Nairobi. This private facility offers slaughter and meat processing services. The demand for pigs is quite high in this facility.

Ndumboini Farm slaughter house

This abattoir is located in Kikuyu, Kiambu county and receives pigs from all over the country. It is one of the main suppliers to butcheries in Nairobi.

Lyntano slaughter house

This facilty is located in the nothern outskirts of Nairobi.

Kabati slaughter house

Kabati abattoir is located in Murang'a South district.Cattle,sheep and goats are slaughtered in one section while pigs in another section. The abattoir receives pigs from Murang'a and surrounding counties like Nyeri and Thika .

Other slaughter houses/slabs

Large and medium pig commercial enterprises have their own slaughter slabs where they slaughter for themselves and those of neighbouring small scale farmers. In major towns and rural districts there are various types of slaughter slabs.
